“Xiang Shu ”




XIANG SHU is my very first emoji character, inspired by a gift from a friend - a plush elephant toy. Its Chinese name, "象鼠" (xiàngshǔ), literally translates to "elephant rat," which in Chinese also sounds like "xiǎng sǐ," meaning "want to die." The inspiration behind it came from a quirky catchphrase I often use in my daily life, a cute and exaggerated way of expressing things. So, XIANG SHU is essentially a combination of a melancholic and humorous creature.
